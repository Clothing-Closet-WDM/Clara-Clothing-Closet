Free Clothes to Kids in Central Iowa

Open 5:30-7:00 PM on Tuesdays and Thursdays for access to various donated clothing items. Located at 815 8th Street at Imani Family Church, Clara's Clothing Closet is helping young people in the West Des Moines area find clothes that best fit their style. Teens and Young Adults can enter in the front entrance or knock if using the back entrance.
